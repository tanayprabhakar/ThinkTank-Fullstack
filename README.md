# ThinkTank-fullstack
A software engineering project to observe the workflow of a company and how a team collaborates to manage tasks and work together effectively 

### **Frontend**  
- **Next.js 15 & React 19** – For building a fast and scalable frontend  
- **TailwindCSS & ShadcnUI** – For styling and UI components  

### **Backend & API**  
- **tRPC** – Type-safe APIs for seamless frontend-backend communication  
- **Mux** – Real-time video processing and streaming  

### **Database & Storage**  
- **PostgreSQL** – A robust relational database  
- **DrizzleORM** – For efficient and type-safe database management  

### **AI & Automation**  
- **AI-powered title and description generation** – Enhancing content metadata  
- **Automatic video transcription** – Improving accessibility and searchability  
- **Smart thumbnail generation** – AI-driven thumbnail selection  

### **Features & Functionality**  
- **Advanced video player** – With quality controls  
- **Creator Studio** – Providing analytics and insights  
- **Custom playlist management** – Organizing video content  
- **Multiple content feeds** – Different viewing experiences  
- **Interactive comment system** – User engagement  
- **Like and subscription system** – Enhancing user interaction  
- **Watch history tracking** – Personalized viewing experience  

### **Security & Architecture**  
- **Authentication system** – User management and access control  
- **Module-based architecture** – For scalability and maintainability  

### **Performance & Responsiveness**  
- **Responsive design** – Optimized across devices
